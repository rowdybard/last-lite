📚 How to Play

  • Shared World: Everyone plays in the same world - you'll see other players!
  • Parties: Form parties with friends to tackle dungeons together
  • Movement: Use "move north/south/east/west" or click the buttons
  • Chat: Use "say [message]" to talk to other players
  • Combat: Use "attack [target]" to fight monsters
  • Parties: Use "party create", "party invite [name]", "party join [name]"
  • Dungeons: Use "dungeon enter [name]" to start a dungeon with your party
